5. Microsoft Advertising (Bing Ads)

5.1 UET Tag (Universal Event Tracking)

We use Microsoft’s UET tag on our Website. It collects information such as pages visited, actions taken (e.g., purchases, form submissions), and metadata about your device/browser. This allows us to track conversions and optimize campaigns.

5.2 Remarketing and Retargeting

With your consent, Microsoft may use cookies to display targeted ads across Bing, MSN, Outlook.com, and partner sites. You may see ads for Printer Care Pro.com after visiting our Website.

5.3 Consent & Control

We do not load UET or marketing cookies for EU/UK visitors until you give explicit consent via our cookie banner. You can withdraw or change your consent at any time. Microsoft acts as an independent data controller for its advertising services.

6. Cookies & Consent Management

6.1 Cookie Categories

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for site operation (cannot be turned off).
  • Functional Cookies: Enable features like remembering preferences.
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect usage statistics to improve services.
  • Advertising/Marketing Cookies: Enable retargeting, ad personalization, and audience measurement.

6.2 Consent Mechanism

A cookie banner is displayed to EU/UK visitors. You may Accept All, Reject All, or Customize Settings. Your preferences are stored and respected on subsequent visits. You may update or withdraw consent anytime via “Cookie Settings” in the Website footer.

6.3 Opt-Out Options

At Digiprintexpert, we prioritize your privacy and give you the option to opt out of sharing your personal information. If you wish to opt out, you can do so by following these steps:

  • Email Request: Send an email to info@printercarepro.shop with the subject line "Opt-Out Request" and include your name and email address associated with your account.
  • Account Settings: If applicable, log into your account and update your privacy preferences to restrict data sharing.
  • Third-Party Sharing: If you want to opt out of sharing your data with third-party partners, please visit our Opt-Out Page (if applicable) or contact us directly.

We will process your request within 7-10 business days. Note that opting out may affect some personalized features of our services. For more details, please review the following:

  • Microsoft opt-out: https://about.ads.microsoft.com
